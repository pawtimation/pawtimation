1. Purpose of This Document

This document defines the exact legal text, structure, and placement required in the footer of all Pawtimation interfaces.
It ensures:
	•	UK legal compliance
	•	GDPR transparency
	•	ICO-friendly clarity
	•	Consistency across all environments
	•	Readiness for business customers and regulators
	•	Safe deployment to Replit

This footer replaces all previous versions.

⸻

2. Mandatory Footer Content

Every public-facing and logged-in page must contain the following links and statements.

Required Legal Links
	1.	Terms of Service
	2.	Privacy Policy
	3.	Cookie Policy
	4.	Data Protection Statement
	5.	Data Processing Agreement (for businesses)
	6.	Beta Tester Addendum (during beta)

All links must be:
	•	visible
	•	accessible without login
	•	available on all devices
	•	functioning URLs

7. Placement & Format Requirements

To ensure compliance:
	•	Footer must appear at the bottom of every page
	•	Font size must be readable (minimum 12px equivalent)
	•	Links must be accessible without login
	•	Must not be hidden behind menus
	•	Cannot require scrolling behind infinite scroll content
	•	Must be visible on mobile and desktop
	•	Must render properly in dark mode and light mode

⸻

8. Technical Implementation Requirements for Replit

Replit must:
	1.	Remove all previous address entries
	2.	Replace footer components in:
	•	apps/web/src/components/Footer.jsx
	•	apps/web/src/components/MarketingFooter.jsx (if exists)
	•	apps/web/src/components/AuthFooter.jsx
	•	any shared footer components
	3.	Replace any older legal files with the new versions
	4.	Ensure dynamic copyright year is implemented
	5.	Ensure links point to the new legal page paths
	6.	Ensure mapping disclaimers are removed
	7.	Display beta disclaimer while in beta
	8.	Confirm no old addresses remain in:
	•	Terms
	•	Privacy
	•	Cookie Policy
	•	Data Protection Statement
	•	Data Processing Agreement
	•	Marketing site copy
	•	Emails (sendClientWelcomeEmail, etc.)
	•	System templates

⸻

9. Required Audit After Implementation

After Replit applies these updates, Pawtimation Partner AI must:
	•	scan all files for old addresses
	•	inspect all footers for accuracy
	•	check all links resolve
	•	confirm consistency across portals
	•	verify dynamic copyright
	•	validate that no stale legal text remains

A full-site audit will be completed once Replit finishes.
