# Platform Owner Terms of Service

**Last Updated**: 26 November 2025  
**Applies to**: Platform Owner (Super Admin) Access

## Overview

These terms govern access to the Pawtimation Super Admin Portal ("Owner Portal"). The Owner Portal provides system-wide administration capabilities including business management, error monitoring, and platform health oversight.

---

## 1. Access and Authentication

### 1.1 Authorised Users

Access to the Owner Portal is restricted to:
- Andrew James Beattie (Platform Owner)
- Designated representatives with explicit authorisation

### 1.2 Security Requirements

Owner accounts must:
- Use strong, unique passwords (minimum 16 characters)
- Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
- Complete MFA challenge for sensitive operations
- Maintain session security (automatic timeout after inactivity)

## 2. Owner Responsibilities

### 2.1 Data Protection

The Platform Owner agrees to:
- Process personal data in accordance with UK GDPR
- Maintain confidentiality of all business and user data
- Implement appropriate technical and organisational measures
- Report and respond to data breaches within required timeframes
- Maintain records of processing activities

### 2.2 Platform Management

The Platform Owner is responsible for:
- Ensuring platform availability and reliability
- Maintaining security patches and updates
- Responding to support requests
- Managing beta programme operations
- Handling GDPR data subject requests

---

## 3. Access to Business Data

### 3.1 Scope of Access

The Owner Portal provides access to:
- Business registrations and subscription status
- Aggregated usage statistics
- Error logs and system health metrics
- Beta tester management
- Support escalations

### 3.2 Limitations

Owner access shall:
- Be used only for legitimate platform operations
- Not be used to access individual booking or financial details without cause
- Respect business isolation principles
- Maintain audit trails for all administrative actions

---

## 4. Prohibited Actions

The Platform Owner shall not:
- Access or modify individual user data without legitimate business reason
- Share login credentials with unauthorised persons
- Disable security features including MFA
- Retain backup access outside secure systems
- Use platform data for personal benefit
