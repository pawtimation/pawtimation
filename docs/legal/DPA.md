# DATA PROCESSING AGREEMENT (DPA)

**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** 26 November 2025

**Parties:**

(1) Pawtimation, operated by Andrew James Beattie, trading as a sole trader  
Registered business address:  
Lytchett House, 13 Freeland Park, Wareham Road, Poole, Dorset, BH16 6FA, United Kingdom

(2) The Business using the Pawtimation platform (the "Controller")

This Data Processing Agreement ("Agreement") forms part of the Terms of Service between the parties and governs Pawtimation's processing of personal data on behalf of the Business.

---

## 1. Definitions

**Controller:** The business using Pawtimation to manage client, staff and pet information.  
**Processor:** Pawtimation.  
**Data Protection Laws:** UK GDPR, the Data Protection Act 2018, and all applicable UK privacy laws.  
**Personal Data:** Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.  
**Processing:** Any operation performed on personal data.  
**Sub-Processor:** A third party engaged by Pawtimation to process personal data.  
**Services:** The Pawtimation CRM platform.

---

## 2. Roles of the Parties

The Controller determines the purposes and means of processing.
Pawtimation, as Processor, processes personal data only on documented instructions from the Controller.

Nothing in this Agreement changes the Controller's obligations under UK GDPR.

---

## 3. Nature and Purpose of Processing

Pawtimation processes personal data for the provision of CRM tools including:
- managing client and pet profiles
- managing bookings
- staff management
- messaging
- generating invoices
- storing dog photographs
- operational record keeping

No mapping, geocoding, GPS tracking or location-based processing is performed.

---

## 4. Categories of Data Subjects

Controller Data Subjects include:
- Clients (pet owners)
- Staff
- Business owners
- Emergency contacts
- Pets (non-personal data, but stored alongside personal data)

---

## 5. Categories of Personal Data Processed

- Names
- Addresses (text only)
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Emergency contact details
- Veterinary information (basic, user-entered)
- Dog details (name, breed, behaviour notes, age, photos)
- Booking details
- Messages
- Invoices and service summaries
- Authentication data (hashed passwords, emails)

Pawtimation does not process:
- GPS coordinates
- Geolocation data
- Biometric data
- Special category data intentionally

---

## 6. Duration of Processing

Pawtimation will process personal data:
- for as long as the Controller uses the platform, and
- thereafter in accordance with retention periods specified in the Privacy Policy.

At termination, personal data will be deleted or returned to the Controller, subject to legal obligations to retain financial records.

---

## 7. Processor Obligations

Pawtimation shall:

1. Process personal data only on documented instructions from the Controller.
2. Ensure individuals with access to personal data are bound by confidentiality obligations.
3. Implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures as required under Article 32 UK GDPR.
4. Not engage any Sub-Processor without ensuring equivalent contractual protections.
5. Assist the Controller with data subject rights requests.
6. Assist with DPIAs and consultations as required.
7. Notify the Controller of any personal data breach without undue delay.
8. Delete or return personal data following termination of services.
9. Make available all information necessary for demonstrating compliance.

Pawtimation will not:
- use personal data for advertising
- sell personal data
- process personal data for its own purposes outside those described in this Agreement

---

## 8. Controller Obligations

The Controller shall:

1. Ensure it has a lawful basis for all processing.
2. Provide appropriate privacy notices to its clients and staff.
3. Ensure personal data entered into Pawtimation is accurate.
4. Manage consent where required.
5. Handle all data subject rights requests related to Controller data.
6. Not upload excessive or unnecessary personal data.
7. Ensure staff use the Service in accordance with the Terms and this Agreement.

---

## 9. Sub-Processors

Pawtimation uses the following Sub-Processors:

| Sub-Processor | Purpose | Location |
|---------------|---------|----------|
| Replit | Hosting infrastructure | United States |
| Neon | PostgreSQL database service | United States / EU |
| Resend | Transactional email delivery | United States |
| Stripe | Payment processing (disabled during beta) | EU/US |

Pawtimation ensures all Sub-Processors:
- operate under legally enforceable contracts
- provide adequate protection (SCCs + UK Addendum)
- implement suitable security controls

Pawtimation may update the list of Sub-Processors and will notify controllers where such updates materially affect processing.

---

## 10. International Transfers

International transfers occur to the United States and EU for hosting and email delivery.

Transfers are safeguarded by:
- UK Addendum to SCCs
- Technical and organisational measures
- Access control
- Encryption in transit
- Secure hosting environments

No international transfers occur outside approved Sub-Processors.

---

## 11. Security Measures

Pawtimation implements:
- TLS encryption
- HSTS
- Content Security Policy (no mapping or tile domains)
- CORS restrictions
- AES-256-GCM encryption for sensitive data
- Password hashing
- Rate limiting
- Sanitised logging
- Role-based access control
- Database isolation
- Secure environment variable management
- Zero location/GPS processing

Details are documented in the Security Measures Overview.

---

## 12. Data Breach Notification

If Pawtimation becomes aware of a personal data breach involving Controller data, it will:

1. Notify the Controller without undue delay.
2. Provide details of the breach, likely impact, and mitigation steps.
3. Take all reasonable measures to contain and resolve the incident.
4. Support the Controller in making any required regulatory notifications.

---

## 13. Data Subject Rights Assistance

Pawtimation will assist the Controller by providing appropriate tools and information to fulfil rights requests, including:
- access
- rectification
- deletion
- restriction
- data portability
- objection

Requests related to Controller data will be referred to the Controller.

---

## 14. Return or Deletion of Data

Upon termination:
- The Controller may request export of its data.
- Pawtimation will delete all data except where retention is required by law.
- Financial records will be retained for seven years.

---

## 15. Audits and Inspections

Pawtimation will:
- provide information necessary to demonstrate compliance
- reasonably assist during audits
- restrict access to sensitive operational information where necessary for security

Remote assessment is preferred unless regulatory requirements mandate otherwise.

---

Nothing in this Agreement limits liability for:
- death or personal injury caused by negligence
- fraud
- anything that cannot be lawfully excluded

## 17. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales.
Disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of England and Wales.
