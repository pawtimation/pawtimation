# Cancellation and Refund Policy

**Last Updated**: 26 November 2025  
**Applies to**: Pawtimation Subscription Services

---

## Overview

This policy explains how subscriptions to Pawtimation can be cancelled and what happens to your data and access after cancellation.

---

## 1. Subscription Cancellation

### 1.1 How to Cancel

You can cancel your Pawtimation subscription at any time by:
- Going to Settings > Billing in your admin dashboard
- Clicking "Manage Subscription" to access the Stripe customer portal
- Selecting "Cancel Subscription"

Alternatively, contact us at pawtimation.uk@gmail.com.

### 1.2 Cancellation Timing

- Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period
- You retain full access until the end of your paid period
- No partial refunds are provided for unused time within a billing period

---

## 2. Beta Programme

### 2.1 Beta Cancellation

During the beta period:
- You may stop using the service at any time
- No payment is required during beta
- Your data can be exported upon request

### 2.2 Transition to Paid

When beta ends:
- You will be notified at least 14 days in advance
- You may choose to subscribe or export your data
- Accounts not converted within 30 days may be suspended

---

## 3. Refunds

### 3.1 General Policy

Pawtimation subscriptions are generally non-refundable. This includes:
- Monthly subscription fees
- Annual subscription fees
- Plan upgrades

### 3.2 Exceptions

Refunds may be considered for:
- Service outages exceeding 48 hours
- Billing errors or duplicate charges
- Demonstrable platform defects preventing use
- First-time users within 7 days of initial subscription

### 3.3 How to Request

To request a refund, contact pawtimation.uk@gmail.com with:
- Your business name and email
- Reason for refund request
- Date of the charge in question

Requests are reviewed within 5 business days.

---

## 4. After Cancellation

### 4.1 Data Access

After your subscription ends:
- **30 days**: Read-only access to export data
- **90 days**: Data retained but account suspended
- **90+ days**: Data deleted unless legal retention applies

### 4.2 Data Export

Before or after cancellation, you can request:
- Full data export (JSON format)
- Client and pet records
- Booking history
- Invoice records

Request exports at pawtimation.uk@gmail.com.

### 4.3 Data Deletion

Upon request or after the retention period:
- Personal data is permanently deleted
- Financial records are anonymised (retained 7 years for tax compliance)
- Media files are removed from storage

---

## 5. Reactivation

### 5.1 Within 90 Days

If you reactivate within 90 days:
- Your data and settings are restored
- You resume from your previous state
- No setup fees apply

### 5.2 After 90 Days

If data has been deleted:
- You start with a fresh account
- Previous data cannot be recovered
- Normal setup process applies

---

## 6. Plan Changes

### 6.1 Upgrades

Plan upgrades:
- Take effect immediately
- Are prorated for the current billing period
- Apply new features immediately

### 6.2 Downgrades

Plan downgrades:
- Take effect at the next billing period
- May result in feature restrictions
- Data is preserved (some features may become read-only)

---

## 7. Cooling Off Period

Under the Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013 (UK):
- You have 14 days to cancel from the date of purchase
- This applies to consumer (non-business) purchases only
- If you have used the service during this period, a pro-rata charge may apply

To exercise your cooling-off right, contact pawtimation.uk@gmail.com within 14 days of purchase.
